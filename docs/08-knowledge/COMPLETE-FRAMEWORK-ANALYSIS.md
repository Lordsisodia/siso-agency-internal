## Executive Summary

I've completed a comprehensive analysis of 11 major AI-driven development frameworks, documenting the unique advantages of each. Each framework wins on specific dimensions that no other framework has.

## Decision Matrix

### Choose BMAD When:
- ✅ Team of 2+ developers
- ✅ Existing codebase (brownfield)
- ✅ Architecture chaos (duplicates)
- ✅ Need consistent methodology
- ✅ Long-term maintenance project

### Choose GSD When:
- ✅ Solo developer
- ✅ Quick prototype
- ✅ Simple task (1-2 files)
- ✅ Speed is priority
- ✅ Blazing fast setup needed

### Choose SpecKit When:
- ✅ Complex requirements
- ✅ Quality-critical systems
- ✅ Regulatory compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2)
- ✅ Team coordination needs
- ✅ Projects where ambiguity is costly

### Choose Ralph When:
- ✅ Long-running autonomous tasks (hours)
- ✅ Multi-iteration workflows
- ✅ Need self-correction
- ✅ Want circuit breaker safety
- ✅ Exit detection critical

### Choose Omo When:
- ✅ Need external data access (docs, examples)
- ✅ IDE-like code navigation needed
- ✅ Cost-sensitive project (multi-model)
- ✅ Long-running background tasks
- ✅ Session history and search needed

### Choose MetaGPT When:
- ✅ Rapid prototyping
- ✅ MVP generation
- ✅ Competitive analysis
- ✅ Learning patterns
- ✅ One-line to complete app

### Choose CrewAI When:
- ✅ Multi-agent workflows
- ✅ Manager-worker architecture needed
- ✅ Output validation critical
- ✅ Conditional branching needed
- ✅ State management required

### Choose AutoGen When:
- ✅ Enterprise Microsoft stack
- ✅ Azure deployment
- ✅ Event-driven architecture needed
- ✅ C# development
- ✅ Compliance required (SOC2, HIPAA)
