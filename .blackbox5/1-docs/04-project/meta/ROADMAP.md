## Risks & Challenges

### Technical Risks
- **Scalability**: Handling growth as user base expands
- **Performance**: Maintaining speed with complex features
- **Data Management**: Ensuring data privacy and portability

### Product Risks
- **User Adoption**: Convincing users to switch from existing tools
- **Feature Overload**: Maintaining simplicity while adding features
- **Domain Complexity**: Managing interdependencies between domains

### Business Risks
- **Competition**: Similar products in productivity space
- **Monetization**: Balancing free and paid features
- **Market Fit**: Ensuring product addresses real user needs
