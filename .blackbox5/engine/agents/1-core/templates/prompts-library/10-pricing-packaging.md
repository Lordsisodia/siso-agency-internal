# Deep Research Prompt 10 — Pricing & packaging (implementation fee vs SaaS fee, module menu)

## CONTEXT

Use the shared Deep Research Context Pack (copy/paste into your run as needed):
- `context-pack.md`

## PROMPT

TASK:
Research how agencies/productized services that look like SaaS package and price offerings in ecommerce + ops tooling.

Deliver:
1) Common pricing models: implementation fee + monthly retainer, per-order fee, per-module fee, revenue share, etc.
2) What competitors charge (where public) and what packaging structures work.
3) A recommended packaging strategy with modules: Catalog Ops, Returns Ops, CX Ops, Growth Ops, Analytics Ops, Platform Ops.
4) A sales narrative: why “unified ops + workflows” is better than stacking apps.

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Use citations for pricing claims.
- End with a proposed pricing page outline + package names.
